HEY THERE! IT'S ME

Welcome to PhotoYo, where the art of storytelling meets the timeless allure of film photography. I'm Judita Cicenaite, the creative force behind the lens. With a passion for capturing raw emotions and unfiltered moments, I specialize in crafting edgy narratives that transcend the ordinary.

My journey through the world of black and white and color photography has led me to the heart of personal narratives, brand identities, pulsating concerts, and the enigmatic realm of music. Each image I create is a piece of visual poetry, a window into the untold stories that dance within the frame.

Drawing inspiration from the interplay of light and shadow, I curate a visual journey that ignites curiosity and evokes emotions. SNAKE SHOT is more than a brand, it's an invitation to explore the uncharted territories of the human experience. Join me as we peel back the layers of existence, one captivating shot at a time.
